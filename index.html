About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Dr. Ayşen Özün Türkçetin. I am a Lecturer at Suleyman Demirel University (SDU). My research interests revolve around Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, and Explainable AI (XAI), focusing primarily on real-world intelligent systems.

I am currently expanding my studies towards multimodal AI and perception for autonomous systems, including large language models (LLMs). I work on applied AI projects including UAV-based perception, thermal imaging, object detection, and biomimetic-inspired designs. I am highly open to international research collaborations in these fields.
